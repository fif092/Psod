Finds the pairs of particles that should be linked through the merging procedure.
two directions are considered simultaneously by the local process:
1= back 2= front
1= left 2= right
1= bottom 2= top
meaning that the local process exchanges information with the processes located in these two directions.

Parameters
[in]nloclocal number of particles flagged for link detection in direction 1
[in]nrecvlocal number of particles flagged for link detection in direction 2
[in]v1neighbour index in direction 1
[in]v2neighbour index in direction 2
[in]pos1positions of local particles flagged for link detection in direction 1
[in]pos2positions of distant particles flagged for link detection in direction 2
[in]r2percolation length
[in]mpicommMPI communicator used in this subroutine
[in]periodic.true. for standard pFoF and .false. for cone version
[in,out]nbridge1number of "bridges" found, e.g. pairs of particles that should be linked, in direction 1
[in,out]nbridge2number of "bridges" found, e.g. pairs of particles that should be linked, in direction 2
[in,out]bridge1array of indices of the particles forming a bridge in direction 1
[in,out]bridge2array of indices of the particles forming a bridge in direction 2
