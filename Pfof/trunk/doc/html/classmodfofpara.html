Initialize the stacks of pointers for local friends of friends.
The whole domain is divided into t^3 cubes, where t is the resolution of the simulation analized times the refine_fof factor.
Each process will implicitly considere only its subdomains because every particles treated by the process is located in the same subdomain.
This routine initializes one stack of "pointers" to the index of the particles located in each cube.

Parameters
[in]nnumber of "cube" in each dimension in a subdomain, i.e. there are n^3 cubes in a subdomain
[in]npnumber of particles in the subdomain
[in]ngppnumber of "FOF" cubes in the subdomain
[in]xpositions of the particles
[in]tresolution of the cosmological simulation times refine_fof factor
[out]npcasenumber of particles in each cube
[out]adfirstindex of the first particle to examine in the cube
[out]pilestack of indices
